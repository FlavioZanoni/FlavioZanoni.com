## **The company:**

SmartFlow is a consulting company, focused on a wide range of clientele, from education systems to medical systems, aims to provide guidance and support on the development of new technologies or partake in the upkeep / improvement of older systems providing next generation tech options and frameworks to always be on top and use the new standards

## **My role:**

As a Full Stack Developer specializing in Internal Tooling at SmartFlow, I play a crucial role in ensuring the smooth operation and continuous improvement of our consultancy services. With a focus on maintaining existing systems and integrating new ones, I contribute to the seamless functioning of diverse client projects.

- \- lead the development of bespoke internal tools, tailored to the specific needs of our clients. These tools optimize workflow processes, enhance productivity, and streamline operations across various sectors, including education and medical systems.
- \- I am responsible for the ongoing maintenance and upkeep of existing systems, ensuring their stability, reliability, and performance. By proactively identifying and addressing issues, I minimize downtime and maximize system efficiency, contributing to client satisfaction and retention.
- \- Pioneered technological solutions within the various Integration practices.
- \- Leveraging my expertise in full stack development, I seamlessly integrate new systems into our existing infrastructure. By collaborating closely with clients and understanding their requirements, I ensure smooth data migration and interoperability, minimizing disruptions and facilitating a seamless transition.
